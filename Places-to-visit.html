The Gilevskaya Roshcha park

The Gilevskaya Roshcha park in Tyumen is one of the favorite places for recreation and walks for citizens. The locals also call it "Gilevka". There is a lot of greenery here, so the park is especially appreciated for the opportunity to take a break from the city with its constant noise and bustle.

Tyumen Ecopark

Ecopark "Zatyumensky" is one of the favorite places for recreation of Tyumen residents. In summer, people walk here with children, ride bicycles and electric scooters, participate in festive events, and in winter the park turns into a skiing area.
